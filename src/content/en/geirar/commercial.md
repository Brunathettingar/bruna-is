Offices, shopping centres and service hubs make up the bulk of urban building stock — and most of them have a basement service tunnel where every utility crosses the same wall and the documentation hasn't been touched in a decade.

We work on new commercial fit-out and, just as often, on bringing older buildings back into compliance: re-sealing penetrations to current EI 60 and EI 90 standards, adding ventilation insulation between tenancies, and producing inspection reports that owners can hand to the fire authority and to prospective buyers.

The work scales — same systems, same documentation — whether the building is a single-tenant office or a thirty-thousand-square-metre mall.
