Fish processing, dairy and meat plants live and die by their cooling chain. Anti-condensation insulation on chilled-water and refrigeration lines is what keeps the floor dry, the product safe and the inspection clean.

We install Armaflex closed-cell foam on cooling pipes, vapour-tight at every joint, alongside insulation on hot CIP and steam lines and fire sealing between process areas and the rest of the building. The hygiene environment is harsh on materials — we specify accordingly and finish to standard.

Cold rooms, freezer stores and ammonia plants are handled with the same documentation set we use on hospital projects.
