Our heavy-industry work centres on the aluminium smelters at Reyðarfjörður, Grundartangi and Straumsvík, alongside silicon plants and the ammonia/methanol producers. These environments wear ordinary insulation out fast: high temperatures around the pot lines, fluoride and dust in the air, and short shutdown windows for any work that touches operations.

We deliver aluminium-clad pipe insulation that holds up outdoors and in the potroom environment, intumescent paint and board protection on the structural steel around the lines, and modular cable transit at every electrical penetration where reliable separation matters.

Documentation is held to the same standard as our hospital work — one register per opening, one report at handover, one point of contact for follow-up over the plant's full operating life.
