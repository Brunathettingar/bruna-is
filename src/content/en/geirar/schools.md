Daycares, primary and secondary schools and the wider stock of public buildings have to be safe, robust and good-looking — children touch every surface, every day. Our pipe insulation is typically finished in Isogenopak: a smooth, dust-free PVC cladding that wipes clean and stays attractive for the life of the building.

Fire sealing follows the same path as commercial work — EI-rated penetrations on every compartment line, with full per-opening documentation that the school's facility manager can hand to inspectors without us in the room.

For older school buildings we typically begin with an inspection report and a remediation plan staged across summer breaks.
