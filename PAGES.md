# Brunaþéttingar — Page-by-page breakdown

The site currently has six HTML pages, plus a stylesheet, an image folder, and a developer brief. The site is in English; the brand name "Brunaþéttingar" stays in the wordmark and as the legal company name throughout. The Icelandic version can be added later via the IS switcher in the utility bar.

All six pages share the same global elements: a thin dark utility bar at the very top (phone, email, IS/EN switch and a "Certified" pill), a sticky white header with the wordmark logo plus a primary nav and an orange "Contact us" button, and a dark footer at the bottom with four columns. Each page also opens with a hero band — full-bleed on the home page and a compact variant on the sub-pages.

The shared primary navigation has seven items: Solutions, Services, Sectors, Quote, About, Articles, and Contact us (the orange CTA).

## 4. Quote calculator — `verdreiknir.html`

Interactive live-calculating quote tool. The functional lead-generation tool of the site.

**Page hero.** Dark hero on the documentation photo. Breadcrumb "Home / Quote calculator". Title "Quote calculator." Kicker explains the three-minute promise.

**How it works intro.** Two-column band. Eyebrow "How it works", headline "An estimated price in three minutes". Right side has a paragraph plus a blue notice box: "This is an estimated price. The final price is confirmed with an on-site inspection. Prices exclude VAT."

**The calculator form.** A bordered panel with:

   - **Header row** (grey background) — column labels: Opening type, Wall type, Fire rating, Width (cm), Height (cm), From floor, Qty, Price.

   - **Calculator rows** — each row is one opening. The user can add unlimited rows. Each row has:
     - **Opening type** dropdown — Cable penetration, Metal pipe, Plastic pipe (intumescent collar), Mixed cables and pipes, Modular cable transit (Roxtec), Linear joint (per m), Ventilation duct.
     - **Wall type** dropdown — Concrete wall, Block/brick wall, Concrete floor, Lightweight wall (gypsum), Lightweight floor.
     - **Fire rating** dropdown — EI 30, EI 60, EI 90, EI 120, EI 240.
     - **Width** and **Height** number inputs in cm.
     - **From floor** dropdown — 0–60 cm (+5% surcharge for hard access), 60–200 cm standard, 200–400 cm (+5%), Over 400 cm (+10%).
     - **Qty** number input.
     - **Live subtotal** showing the row price in ISK with tabular numerals (or a dash if any of the choices are missing).
     - **Remove** × button.

   - **Add a row** button — a dashed-bordered button that adds another empty row.

   - **Total panel** (dark band) — left side has "Estimated total" label and an "Excl. VAT" subline; right side shows the running total in large serif numerals in brand orange.

   - **Customer info form** (continues in the dark band) — heading "Send the estimated quote". A 2×2 grid of inputs (Name, Company/Project name, Email, Phone), plus a Notes textarea. Submit row has a 60% note ("You'll receive a confirmation by email and a project manager will call within 24 hours.") and an orange Submit button. After submit, a green "Thanks!" banner appears with the confirmation message.

**Pricing formula** (implemented in inline JS):

```
subtotal = basePrice × wallMultiplier × fireMultiplier × sizeMultiplier × floorMultiplier × qty
```

For linear joints, the size multiplier is replaced by `(width + height) / 100` to charge per metre of perimeter. The whole formula lives at the top of the inline `<script>` and is easy to tune.

**CTA band.** Two-button CTA: "Request an inspection" and a tel: link to the phone number.
