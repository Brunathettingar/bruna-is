About Brunaþéttingar.

A specialised fire sealing and technical insulation company serving contractors, building owners and operators in Iceland since 2011.

Our story

Founded to seal fire properly — and document it.

Brunaþéttingar was founded in 2011 when two industry workers kept seeing the same problem: penetrations sealed without a report, certifications lost in paperwork, and fire authority inspections being repeated because of missing records.

Our story is a story of documentation. From day one we have insisted that every single seal be tracked, photographed and delivered to the owner in a digital report. When new buildings rise in Iceland we want someone — 30 years from now — to be able to look up the certification of this exact cable seal without ripping open a wall.

Today we are a team of 22, with projects in the capital region, on Reykjanes, the South Coast and the East Fjords, and we serve everything from new commercial buildings to the largest industrial projects in the country.

"Fire sealing is not just the work — it's the report that comes with the work." — Founder, Brunaþéttingar ehf.

Our vision

Six guiding principles for every project.

Our vision is simple: do every project right the first time and document it so the next generation can follow up. These are the rules our crews follow on site and the rules our clients can rely on.

01

Documentation first

No seal goes into a wall without a photo, a location on the drawing and a certification number in our documentation system. The report is not an afterthought — it's part of the work.

02

We don't design — we work well with designers

Fire engineering is not our discipline. We advise on material selection and execution but respect the requirements set by those who designed the project. We specifically recommend Tensio Architects.

03

Certified systems only

No seal made with materials that aren't ETA-certified for the intended use. We use systems from Roxtec, Hilti, Promat, 3M and Beele — never "home-made" solutions.

04

Transparency with the client

Pricing, material choices and project progress are open information. The owner gets access to the documentation system from day one — not just at handover.

05

Continuous training

The whole team attends annual factory training from our manufacturers (Roxtec, Hilti, Promat). The fire sealing trade evolves — we keep up.

06

One project, one report, one inspection

Each project gets one document number, one photo method and one final report. No "we'll add it later" or "we'll come back next week". We finish the job and leave it whole.
