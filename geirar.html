Sectors we serve.

Brunaþéttingar work across the major industrial sectors of Iceland — from power plants and aluminium smelters to hospitals, schools and pharma.

Industry in Iceland

From the Hellisheiði power plant to the hospitals of the capital region.

Technical insulation and fire protection don't look the same in an aluminium smelter as in a hospital. Each sector has its own requirements — chemical resistance in heavy industry, hygiene in food processing, acoustic insulation in schools and hospitals, anti-condensation in the cold rooms of fish processing.

Below is an overview of the sectors we serve with our full seven service areas. Click a sector to see which solutions apply.

Energy & utilities

Geothermal plants, hydropower stations, substations and district heating tunnels. High-temperature insulation, aluminium cladding and fire protection on steam pipelines.

Aluminium & heavy industry

Fishing & food industry

Fish processing plants, dairy and meat processing. Anti-condensation insulation on cooling pipes, Armaflex and fire protection in factories and cold rooms.

  • Cooling pipes

Hospitals & healthcare

Schools & public buildings

  • Fire protection

Commercial & retail

Hotels & residential
